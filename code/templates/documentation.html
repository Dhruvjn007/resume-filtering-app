Documentation

Welcome to the ResumeRank documentation. Here you'll find information on how to use the tool effectively.

How It Works

Our application uses a sophisticated AI model to analyze and rank resumes based on content quality, keyword density, and overall comprehensiveness. The model evaluates resumes for their technical skills, experience depth, and professional presentation.

How to Use

  1. Sign Up/Log In: Create an account to save your work and track candidates.
  2. Upload Resumes: On the "Rank Resumes" page, drag and drop multiple PDF resumes into the upload area.
  3. View Results: Click the "Rank Resumes" button to get a ranked list of candidates based on their resume quality score.

Future Features

  • Shortlisting top candidates to a dashboard.
  • Detailed analysis of keyword matches and misses.
  • Exporting candidate lists to CSV.
